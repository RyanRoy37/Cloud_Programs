What is Cloud Computing?

Cloud computing revolutionizes how we access and use technology resources. It delivers computing services including servers, storage, databases, networking, software, and analytics over the internet.

This paradigm shift enables organizations to innovate faster, reduce costs, and scale resources dynamically based on demand.

Benefits & Advantages

Cloud computing transforms business operations by eliminating upfront infrastructure investments and enabling rapid deployment of new projects.

Organizations benefit from high availability, automatic disaster recovery, global reach, and accelerated time-to-market for their applications and services.
